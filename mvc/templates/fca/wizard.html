FlashChat startup wizard.

Welcome to the adjustments of key parameters FlashChat. With the help of a wizard you can adjust following parameters FlashChat: FlashChat Login Screen, Bad nick and room name, Chat rooms.

FlashChat Login Screen.

Adjustment of parameters of the authorization, the text of rule for use a chat, display of process of loading of the chat.

Chat rooms.

Management of the list of mandatory rooms at loading a chat.

Bad nick and room name.

Adjustment of parameters of a filtration of the forbidden words.

Get config file.

Generation and loading of a file of a chat configuration.
